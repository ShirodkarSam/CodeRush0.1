    Dreams

    When I dream, I go into another world.

    With fairies zipping by.

    Whoosh!

    Unicorns using magic.

    Zap!

    By the ocean, a castle.

    Splash!

    Narwhals playing in rainbow-colored water.

    Mermaids singing a sweet melody.

    Alas, Beep! Beep! Beep!

    The alarm goes off!

    Time to get up!
